CIIT Philippines - BETTER | What does it take to become BETTER?

Core Values: MISSION & VISION We are proud to declare the multimedia arts school mission, vision, and goals that we currently fulfill. Vision Leading the way towards a globally competitive Philippines. Mission We commit to improve Filipino lives by empowering the youth through industry-ready, values-driven and accessible education. Core Values Critical Thinking – We use data, reasoning, and resourcefulness as our tools to understand our targets, formulate strategies and achieve our goals. Integrity – We do the right thing even when no one is looking, even if it is the harder thing to do. Innovation – We seek to continuously improve systems, processes and our way of doing things. Teamwork – We achieve our vision together by resolving conflicts and overcoming barriers. Passion – We commit ourselves to careers we are passionate about and share this with others. We love what we do and do what we love. Led by these values, CIIT Philippines will continue to fulfill its multimedia art mission and vision to become the best digital arts school in the country.
